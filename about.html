ABOUT

ME

Hi! My name is An Nur Hazwani binti Abdul Rauf. I’m currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Media Communication Technology at UiTM Alor Gajah. I enjoy learning about creative digital content whether it’s design, animation, video editing, or anything visual. I love the process of turning simple ideas into something people can see, experience, or connect with.

I’m always curious to learn new things, try out new tools, and find smarter or more creative ways to get things done. I believe in being precise, adaptable, and open to challenges, because that’s where real growth happens. As long as I get to create and express ideas that leave an impact, I’m doing what I love.

In My Free Time

Ever wondered what people do to unwind? Me? I build nano brick models when I get the chance. It might seem small, but there's something really satisfying about turning tiny pieces into something complete. It’s not just a hobby, but a little escape where I get to slow down, focus, and enjoy the process.

How It All Started

Right after SPM,
I took a part-time job managing
a booth that sold bricks. At the
time, it felt like just work
to me.

At the same time, I was looking
for something new to do maybe even a new hobby. I decided to build one of the sets. I didn’t expect much, just
wanted to try.

Ended up really liking it and kept wanting to build more. It slowly turned into one of my favourite hobbies. Every time I finish a model, I feel proud like I have completed a little achievement.

A personal space built by someone who finds calm in building nano bricks.
